# PICO Eyes Ecosystem 👁️🌐
> A Bio-Digital Twin and Multi-Agent Simulation for Animal Vision and Ecosystem Intelligence.

Welcome to the **PICO Eyes Ecosystem**. This repository contains the engine for simulating a "Pandora-style" holographic world space where thousands of AI agents interact based on biological logic, rather than human social sentiment.

## The Vision
Reality is merely the spectrum of data our species is tuned to. **PICO Eyes** shifts that spectrum. 

We are building a scalable, multi-agent environment where the fundamental physical properties of the Earth (Carbon, Nitrogen, Inorganic materials) serve as the "Seed" data. Within this point-cloud ecosystem, AI agents acting as distinct biological species (Insects, Birds, Mammals, Deep Sea Fish) forage, escape, and interact.

## The "Hopper" Mechanic
PICO Eyes introduces the **Self-Hopper** transition. Users can navigate the global holographic view, click on any individual AI agent, and seamlessly transition into that specific species' biological vision model.

* **See the Unseen:** Transition into a Pit Viper to track thermal footprints.
* **Navigate the Spectrum:** Hop into a Bee to see UV-reactive nectar guides.
* **Shift the Reality:** Experience the high-speed, dual-fovea zoom of a Raptor.

## Repository Architecture
* `[ /seeds ]` - Generation scripts for the environmental point-cloud data (the interactive landscape).
* `[ /agents ]` - The swarm-intelligence logic and survival algorithms for our biological personas.
* `[ /shaders ]` - The biomimetic mathematical models converting standard coordinate data into species-specific visual output.
* `[ /web ]` - Client-side rendering integration for seamless browser experiences.
